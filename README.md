# Australia Local Business Scraper

**Extract verified Australian businesses, tradesmen, and local services from Local.com.au.**

The Australia Local Business Scraper is your gateway to extracting premium B2B data from the Australian market. Source highly valuable business leads effortlessly.

## What can Australia Local Business Scraper do?

- ✅ **Extract Australian B2B Leads** - Get business names, physical addresses, and direct contact numbers.
- ✅ **Target specific Industries** - Focus your search on plumbers, tradesmen, accountants, or local services.
- ✅ **Identify Local Footprint** - Target major economic hubs like Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, or Perth.
- ✅ **Export formats** - Download data in JSON, CSV, Excel, or HTML formats.

## Output Format

| Field | Description | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **businessName** | Name of the business | "John's Plumbing" |
| **industry** | Extracted category/industry | "plumber" |
| **address** | The full AU address | "123 Sydney St, NSW" |
| **phone** | Direct contact number | "+61 412 345 678" |
| **website** | Company website | "https://www.example.com.au" |
| **listingUrl** | Link to the directory listing | "https://www.local.com.au/..." |
